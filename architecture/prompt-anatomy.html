From Your Message to Coaching

Every time you ask Ali a question, five things happen behind the scenes -- in under two seconds.

✉

1. You Ask

Your message arrives. Ali extracts key details: who you are negotiating with, what is at stake, where you are in the process. This happens instantly through pattern recognition -- no extra AI calls needed.

⚙

2. Ali Thinks

Ali assesses your situation against the Aligned methodology. What type of negotiation is this? What phase are you in? What is your leverage? This classification determines which coaching strategies are most relevant to you right now.

◈

3. Knowledge Activated

Ali pulls relevant coaching from the Aligned Strategic Framework, 43 Named Moves, and proven negotiation patterns -- matched specifically to your situation. The richer the knowledge base gets, the more precise this matching becomes.

◎

4. Coaching Delivered

Ali gives you 2-3 focused coaching points with a follow-up question. Concise. Actionable. One thread at a time. Each point follows a clear structure: where you are, how it is going, and where to go next.

✓

5. Safety Check

Before you see the response, Ali verifies: no confidential details leaked, pricing stays consistent, and advice remains within coaching scope. Your sensitive information -- price floors, walk-away points, private strategy -- is never exposed in suggested communications.

Who Shapes Ali's Intelligence

The coaching quality comes from a clear division between methodology expertise and engineering automation.

Coaching Partners Author

Methodology architects define what Ali knows and how Ali coaches

  • Coaching philosophy and methodology The Aligned approach: problem-based coaching, not deal-type classification
  • 43 Named Aligned Moves Each move with its definition, trigger, and example coaching line
  • Coaching tone and voice guidelines Conversational authority -- intellectually grounded, never academic
  • Business guardrails What Ali will and will not do -- scope boundaries and redirect patterns
  • Example coaching exchanges Real session examples that set the register for Ali's responses
  • Book review approvals What enters Ali's knowledge base -- every item reviewed and approved

Engineering Handles Automatically

Infrastructure that makes coaching knowledge work at scale

  • Knowledge formatting for the AI model Coaching content wrapped in optimized structure for best AI performance
  • Matching knowledge to each conversation Relevant strategies surfaced based on the user's specific situation
  • User profile personalization Name, role, industry, and context woven into every response
  • Confidentiality protection Dynamic per-conversation guards for sensitive information
  • Response length management Conversation history summarized to stay within optimal context window
  • Cost optimization through caching Stable coaching knowledge cached to reduce per-message costs by 90%

When you approve a coaching item, it automatically becomes available to Ali within the next deployment. No manual updates needed. The coaching partners write and approve methodology content; engineering ensures it flows seamlessly into every conversation.
